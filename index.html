About Me

Hi, I'm Joseph Chafutwa, a passionate developer and problem-solver based in Kenya. With a strong foundation in front-end and fullstack development, I bring web interfaces and applications to life using technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and Flask.
